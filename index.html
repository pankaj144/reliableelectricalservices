What We Do

CORE
SERVICES

From motor rewinding to full substation commissioning, Reliable Electrical Services delivers precision, safety, and compliance across every industrial sector.

01

Motor Rewinding & Overhaul

Complete AC/DC motor rewinding, bearing replacement, insulation testing, and full overhaul for motors from fractional HP to 5,000 HP.

02

VFD & Soft Starter Solutions

Variable frequency drive selection, installation, programming, and commissioning for precise motor speed control and energy savings up to 35%.

03

Motor Control Centers (MCC)

Design, fabrication, and installation of MCC panels with full protection relay coordination, bus bar sizing, and thermal imaging verification.

High-Voltage Installations

05

06

Preventive Maintenance & Audits

Scheduled servicing, thermographic surveys, vibration analysis, insulation resistance testing, and full compliance audits for operational safety.
