Who We Are

TRUSTED
SINCE 1997

Reliable Electrical Services was founded by Mr. Paresh Chandra Saha, a renowned specialized technician with deep expertise in electrical and mechanical engineering. Under his visionary leadership, the company has grown into a premier service provider trusted by India's leading industrial giants.

With nearly three decades of experience, our skilled team of engineers, techno-commercial professionals, and highly trained mechanics work together using advanced tools and well-equipped workshops to deliver world-class service at any industrial site across India.

We are proud partners of Jindal Steel & Power, Tata Group, Vedanta Resources, major national power plants, and leading refineries — relationships built on trust, quality, and consistent on-time delivery.
